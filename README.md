## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing hospital patient admission data to uncover trends, optimize operations, and identify opportunities to increase patient inflow. An interactive Excel dashboard was built to visualize key metrics such as patient demographics, revenue, stay duration, and diagnosis patterns.

---

##  Business Problem Statement
Hospitals often struggle to understand:
- Which patient segments drive most admissions  
- Factors affecting stay duration and revenue  
- Regions and demographics with higher engagement  
- Operational gaps impacting patient inflow  

This project analyzes admission data to generate actionable insights and improve decision-making.

---

##  Dataset Description
The dataset includes:
- Patient demographics (age, gender, location)  
- Admission & discharge details  
- Diagnosis and patient type  
- Insurance status  
- Billing and payment data  

---

##  Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables & Charts  
- Slicers & Filters  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- KPI Design  

---

##  Step-by-Step Process

### 1️ Data Cleaning
- Removed missing and inconsistent values  
- Standardized formats (dates, categories)  
- Structured dataset for analysis  

### 2️ Data Transformation
- Created age groups for segmentation  
- Calculated stay duration  
- Built KPIs:
  - Total Patients  
  - Total Revenue  
  - Avg Stay Duration  

### 3️ Exploratory Data Analysis
- Analyzed patients by gender, age, and region  
- Studied diagnosis and admission trends  
- Compared payment methods and insurance usage  

### 4️ Dashboard Development
Built an interactive Excel dashboard with:
- Total Patients (YoY trend)  
- Patient distribution by state  
- Gender breakdown  
- Avg stay by age group  
- Revenue insights  
- Payment & insurance analysis  
- Diagnosis-based revenue  

---

##  Key Insights (in %)

- Total Patients: 4000  
- Male: 49.4% | Female: 49.0% | Others: 1.6%  

- Insurance Used: 71%  

###  Payment Methods
- Cash: 40.5%  
- Credit Card: 30.0%  
- UPI: 29.5%  

###  Trends
- Stable admission trend with slight monthly variation  
- Peak admissions in year-end months  

###  Revenue
- Total Revenue: ₹78M+  
- Avg Revenue per Patient: ₹1.88L  
- Top treatment contributes ~20%+ revenue  

###  Patient Behavior
- Avg Stay: 8 days  
- Majority patients in 18–45 age group

## Recommendations to Increase Admissions

### Target High-Demand Regions
Focus marketing on high inflow states/cities  

### Improve Insurance Partnerships
Expand coverage and promote cashless options  
→ Reduces financial barriers  

### Optimize High-Demand Treatments
Invest in frequently used departments  

### Reduce Stay Duration
Improve operations for faster discharge  
→ More bed availability  

### Age-Based Services
Create packages for dominant age groups  

###  Enhance Payment Experience
Support UPI, cards, insurance  
Simplify admission process
